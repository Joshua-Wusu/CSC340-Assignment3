Welcome to the Bird Gallery! This website was created for an assignment, birds were chosen as the main theme for the gallery. The web page presents 6 unique and diverse birds from around the world.

This was a rather fun and not so tedious assignment, I enjoyed it quite a lot. HTML was one of the very first markup language I dabbled in growing up, though I had long forgotten its inner workings, it was nice discovering that lost skill once again.
